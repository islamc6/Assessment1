Must Do's of Glasgow

Discover some historical insights and must-do activities of Glasgow

Visit The Glasgow Cathedral

A stunning medieval cathedral with rich history and incredible architecture. Explore the intricate stained glass windows, impressive stone carvings, and peaceful surroundings in the heart of Glasgow.

Learn More
Explore Kelvingrove Art Gallery & Museum

A must-see museum with diverse collections from art to natural history. Spend a few hours exploring the exhibits and beautiful surrounding park.

Learn More
Walk Around the Glasgow Green

A historic park with plenty of green space for walking, picnicking, or enjoying the nearby monuments. A perfect spot for relaxation.

Learn More
Vintage Bus Tour with Afternoon Tea or Gin

See the best of Glasgow as you ride aboard a classic red double-decker on a guided bus tour. Enjoy an afternoon tea experience with traditional British treats and sip on optional gin cocktails.

Learn More
Jacobite Steam Train & The Highlands Tour

Discover Scotland’s magical Highlands, explore historic towns, and ride the famous Jacobite Steam Train — Hogwarts Express from the Harry Potter films — crossing the iconic Glenfinnan Viaduct.

Learn More
City Center Guided Walking Tour

Discover the best of Glasgow on a guided walking tour with a local guide. See Glasgow's key sights and venture off the beaten path to discover street art and more.

Learn More

There are many more exciting things to do in Glasgow. Make sure to explore and enjoy the city!
